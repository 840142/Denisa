Denisa Štefanigová

Denisa Štefanigová (b. 1995)

Czech visual artist and painter based in Brno. She studied painting in Brno and Estonia.

She has completed residencies in Bilbao, Stuttgart, Graz, Prague and Budapest, and exhibited internationally.

Finalist of the Cyprián Prize (2024) and Young Painter Prize (2021).
